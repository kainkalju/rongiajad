Features

GPS-based nearest stop

The app automatically detects your location and shows upcoming departures from the nearest train stop.

Full Elron timetable

Browse weekday, Saturday, and Sunday schedules for all Elron lines across Estonia.

Search stops and lines

Quickly find any stop or line by name. Results show all routes serving that stop.

Favourites

Save your most-used stops and lines for instant access from the home screen.

Trip stop sequence

Tap any departure to see the full list of stops with times. Your current stop is highlighted.

Works offline

All schedule data is bundled with the app. No internet connection needed to view timetables.
