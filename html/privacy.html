Privacy Policy

Rongiajad — Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Rongiajad is a train schedule app for Elron services in Estonia. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Rongiajad does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or process any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Crash reports sent to any server
  • Location history
  • Any data shared with third parties

Location Access

The app requests access to your device location solely to find the nearest train stop and show upcoming departures. Location data is used in real time on your device only and is never stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

Local Storage

Favourites (saved stops and lines) are stored locally on your device using the system's standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Schedule Data Updates

The app includes train schedule data bundled at install time. An optional in-app update can download a fresh schedule file from a public Elron GTFS feed. This request contains no personal information and no usage data is sent along with it.

Children's Privacy

Rongiajad does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
